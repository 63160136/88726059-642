Kokomi

Sangonomiya Kokomi (Japanese: 珊さん瑚ご宮のみや心ここ海み Sangonomiya Kokomi) is a playable Hydro character in Genshin Impact. The young Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island and a descendant of the Sangonomiya Clan. Kokomi is in charge of most of Watatsumi's affairs, shouldering heavy responsibilities alone in hopes for giving Watatsumi Island's people the hopes and happiness that they desire. The Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island. All of the island's affairs are at this young lady's fingertips.
desire to protect Watatsumi Island lives on in the bloodline of the Sangonomiya Clan. The current heir to this bloodline is known as the Divine Priestess. The meaning behind this is as such: to inherit the will of a god in a mortal's body, and to defend this land and its people in that god's stead.
